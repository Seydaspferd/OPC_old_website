Obrobibini Peace Complex
For More Inner Peace

White meets Black, Black meets White

Obrobibini is a mix between two words in Twi (local language in Ghana): "Obroni" means "white man", "Obibini" means "black man". Obrobibini represents the union of people from all origins. Our regenerative learning centres are places for people from different cultures to learn from each other, the kind of environments we need for a decent human life with a healthy body and mind.
