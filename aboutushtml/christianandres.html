Dr. Christian Andres


Christian is the President and Founding Member of OPC Switzerland and Executive Director of OPC Ghana. He is a tropical agronomist, and did his Bachelor, Master and PhD in sustainable agriculture in West Africa (Bachelor & Master in Ivory Coast, PhD in Ghana (Dr sc. ETH)). During the time of his PhD, Christian initiated Obrobibini. His core competencies are project management, organic agriculture, sports and spirituality/meditation.
