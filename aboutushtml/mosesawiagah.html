Moses Awiagah


Moses Awiagah is the Board Secretary and second lead farmer of OPC Ghana. He graduated from the College of Agricultural Sciences and worked at the Cocoa Research Institute of Ghana (CRIG) with the President of OPC Switzerland as part of his doctoral research. His ability to put himself at the service of others, as well as his gift for understanding people from different backgrounds, make him a great team player. His core competencies are agriculture and project man- agement.
